EcoTwin — AI Environmental Digital Twin

Built at Hack4impact. Ingests live air quality data from NASA and OpenAQ, trains XGBoost models to forecast Delhi's AQI up to 24 hours ahead, and predicts pollution hotspots across a city grid — all on a live heatmap dashboard.

// what I learned: an ML model is 20% of the work. Getting real data into it without breaking everything is the other 80%.
